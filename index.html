Our Mission

SSD Matrix Labs' primary mission is to restore the appearance and value of bank notes that have been damaged, stained or altered. Using specialized techniques and procedures, this laboratory removes stains and impurities from banknotes, allowing them to be put back into circulation or returned to their original owner. Cleaning processes may vary depending on the type of damage the notes have suffered, but the end goal is to make them usable and in good condition again.
